# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard 📊

## Executive Summary
This project transforms raw HR data into actionable retention strategies using Power BI. By analyzing a workforce of 1,473 employees, this dashboard uncovers the "why" behind a **16.1% attrition rate**, providing leadership with a multi-dimensional view of employee movement to reduce recruitment costs and stabilize the workforce.

## 📌 Situation
The organization faced rising turnover, leading to lost productivity and diminished institutional knowledge. Without a centralized data view, leadership lacked clarity on which departments or demographics were most at risk, making it difficult to implement effective retention policies.

## 🎯 Task
My goal was to design and implement a comprehensive **HR Attrition Dashboard** to:
*   Identify high-risk corridors across salary brackets, age groups, and job roles.
*   Provide a data-driven roadmap for targeted HR interventions.
*   Visualize complex workforce trends for executive decision-making.

## 🛠️ Actions Taken
I processed complex datasets to create a high-impact visual narrative focusing on four key pillars:

1.  **Segmented Attrition:** Analyzed exits by Gender (143 Male vs. 80 Female) and Education Field, identifying *Life Sciences* as the most affected area (37.5%).
2.  **Financial Impact:** Correlated attrition with compensation, revealing that **68% of leavers** were in the "Upto 5k" salary slab.
3.  **Role-Specific Mapping:** Identified *Laboratory Technicians* and *Sales Executives* as high-churn roles requiring immediate attention.
4.  **Lifecycle Analysis:** Used a "Years at Company" trend line to pinpoint the **2-year mark** as a critical period for employee disengagement.

## 📈 Key Results & Insights
*   **Demographic Hotspots:** Discovered that the **26-35 age group** accounts for nearly 50% of all exits, highlighting a need for better mid-career engagement.
*   **Departmental Prioritization:** Provided HR with a clear mandate to review *Research & Development* and *Sales* departments for cultural or compensatory adjustments.
*   **Operational Clarity:** The dashboard enables proactive rather than reactive leadership, targeting specific "high-risk" employee profiles before they exit.

## 🚀 Technologies Used
*   **Power BI:** Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive visualization.
*   **Excel/CSV:** Source data processing and cleaning.
